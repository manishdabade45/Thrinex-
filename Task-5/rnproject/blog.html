Welcome to the official blog of R.N. Agritech Services! We are thrilled to share our passion for agriculture, landscaping, and sustainable green spaces with you.

Who We Are

R.N. Agritech Services is a premier provider of comprehensive landscape gardening and agricultural consultancy services based in Pune, Maharashtra. With over 15 years of rich experience, we have successfully completed 1000+ projects and brought smiles to more than 500 happy clients.

Our Expertise

Our core services encompass a wide range of horticultural solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients:

  • Landscape Gardening: From conceptual design to meticulous execution, we create breathtaking outdoor spaces.
  • Plantation Work: Expert planting techniques and care to ensure the healthy growth of your trees and plants.
  • Agricultural Consultancy: Professional advice on soil health, crop selection, and irrigation systems to maximize your yield.
  • Pest Control Services: Safe and effective pest management to protect your green investments.
  • Horticultural Materials & Plants: We supply a diverse range of premium plants, nutrient-rich soil, organic fertilizers, and other essential gardening materials.

Why Choose R.N. Agritech?

We pride ourselves on our certified team of professionals who are dedicated to delivering high-quality, timely, and cost-effective solutions. Whether you're looking to transform a small backyard or manage a large-scale agricultural project, we have the expertise and resources to turn your vision into reality.

Stay tuned to this blog for expert tips on plant care, landscaping trends, and the latest news from R.N. Agritech Services. We look forward to growing together!
