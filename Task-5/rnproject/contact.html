Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Pune and the surrounding areas in Maharashtra. For large projects, we can also travel to nearby districts.

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your garden or landscape remains healthy and beautiful year-round.

We prioritize the use of safe, organic pest control solutions whenever possible. We always inform you of any precautions needed after a treatment.
